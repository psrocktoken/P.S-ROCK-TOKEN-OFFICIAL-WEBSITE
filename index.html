What is PS Rock?

PS Rock is a crypto token deployed on Binance Smart Chain following the BEP20 standard. It allows peer-to-peer transfers, on-chain balance tracking, and transparent ownership. The project aims to provide a clean, understandable digital asset without unnecessary complexity.

Decentralized

Runs on BSC blockchain with immutable smart contract logic.

Secure

Solidity 0.8.x with overflow protection and owner-controlled metadata.

Transparent

All supply and balances are publicly verifiable on-chain.
